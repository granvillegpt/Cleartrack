Password Reset Flow - Step by Step

This page shows what users will see at each step of the password reset process

Step 1: User clicks "Forgot password?" on Login Page
User is on the login page and clicks the "Forgot password?" link below the password field.
ClearTrack Logo
Login

Sign in to your ClearTrack account to view your travel logbook, vehicles, and tax documents.

Forgot password?
Step 2: Password Reset Email Sent
After clicking "Forgot password?", a confirmation alert appears (or the email is sent directly).
ClearTrack Logo
Login
Password reset email sent!

Please check your inbox and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Step 3: User Receives Email
User receives an email from Firebase with a password reset link.
From: noreply@cleartrack.firebaseapp.com
To: user@example.com
Subject: Reset your password for ClearTrack

You requested to reset your password for your ClearTrack account.

Click the button below to reset your password:

Reset Password

If you didn't request this, you can safely ignore this email.

Step 4: Reset Password Page (Opened Directly - No Token)
If user opens the reset password page directly without the email link, they see this message.
ClearTrack Logo
Reset Password
Please use the password reset link from your email to reset your password. If you don't have the link, request a new one.
Step 5: Reset Password Page (With Valid Token from Email)
User clicks the link in the email and lands on the reset password page with the form visible.
ClearTrack Logo
Reset Password

Enter your new password below.

Step 6: Password Successfully Reset
After submitting the form with matching passwords, the success notification appears.
ClearTrack Logo
Reset Password

Enter your new password below.

Password successfully reset!

Redirecting to login page...

Step 7: Redirected to Login Page
After 2 seconds, user is automatically redirected back to the login page to sign in with their new password.
ClearTrack Logo
Login

Sign in to your ClearTrack account to view your travel logbook, vehicles, and tax documents.

Forgot password?
